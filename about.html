About Me

Q: Where are you from?

A: I'm from Columbia, Missouri. I attended Hallsville High School until I graduated in 2016. I then attended the University of Missouri where I majored in statistics until I graduated in May of 2020 with a B.S. in Statistics with a minor in Computer Science. I am now attended Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, in pursuit of a Masters degree in Computer Science. I am interested in pursuing the field of artificial intelligence, computer vision, application development, and computer graphics.

Q: What are you passionate about?

A: I am passionate about solving problems using advanced machine learning principals. The application of these things can vastly improve the way we travel, eat, sleep, entertain, and ultimately live. Anyone who knows me knows that I enjoy fishing, sports (basketball and football mainly), and the occasional video game. I am also widely known throughout my friends and family for wearing a bright yellow Nike hat in every moment of my free time. Ultimately I am most passionate about spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ. The gospel has brought so much joy to my life, and I love being able to show others the wonderful plan that Heavenly Father has for us. Kindess is a principal that I try to live my everyday life by.
